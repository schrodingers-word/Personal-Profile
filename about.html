About Me

My name is Pako Fain Mojuta and I am currently studying Computer Systems Engineering at Botswana Accountancy College (now Botswana School of Business Sciences).

I have a strong interest in industrial automation, PLC systems, and industrial instrumentation. I enjoy learning how machines, control systems, and sensors work together in modern industrial environments.

My career goal is to become a fully qualified Control Systems Engineer and work in industries that rely on automation and intelligent systems.

Besides programming, I also enjoy practical technical work and have experience using workshop machinery such as welding equipment, grinders, and other tools.
