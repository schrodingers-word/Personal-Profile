About Me

My name is Pako Fain Mojuta and I am studying Computer Systems Engineering at Botswana Accountancy College, now known as Botswana School of Business Sciences.

I am passionate about industrial automation, PLC systems, and industrial instrumentation. I enjoy learning how automated systems operate in real industrial environments such as manufacturing plants and mining operations.

My goal is to become a Control Systems Engineer and work in industries where automation and technology are used to improve efficiency.

In addition to programming, I also have practical workshop skills including welding, grinding, and working with mechanical tools. This allows me to understand both the software and physical sides of engineering systems.
